3 EASY STEPS

Wash Paint First

Clay Bar The Surface

Dry Car

×

Step 1

For the paint use a dedicated soap. Paint soaps are ph neutral which means it won't strip away are cars surface. Some soaps even have wax included to give the surface extra protect. Thought the protection won't last long it's still helpful. Aways start at the top of the cars surface and leave the bottom for last(bottom has more dirt). Many people use wash mitts, however, wash mops allow you the reach the top of SUVS and lower portions without bending.

Step 2

After washing the paint surface you will need to clay. Clay removes the embeded contamination not removed by the soap. To begin use the same soap cleaner from step one and rub the clay bar left-right. (East-West) Not in circles because of chance of maring. You move the clay to another section when you fell the surface getting smooter. You only clay when you will follow with some sort of protection after. Optional use iron remover before clay bar. Clay bars come in many forms, pick the one you like the best.

Step 3

Drying the car is important to prevent water spots. You can either use a towel with spray waz and buff dry. Or use a leaf blower. The advantage of using a leaf blower is that it is touch less. Therefore, no scratches.
